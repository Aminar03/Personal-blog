Embracing Growth:My Upskill Journey

Posted on:10 June 2023

It's my first time writing a blog.Hope you will enjoy it!!!

I am here to share my experience about Upskill, a incredible initiative organized by the TinkerHub Society of our college.
Upskill has truly been a game-changer, providing a platform for students to dive into the fascinating world of technical studies, such as web development, Flutter, and more. Under the guidance of our seniors, this program has paved the way for endless possibilities and personal growth.
From March 13 onwards our collage declared a vacation of one month. We were so excited for our vacation .Then after somedays I think it is better to do something than wasting time. So me and my friends decided to join in Upskill.
We chose Web Developement as our topic.Initially I had no idea about it.But It was such a great experience.I learned html & css from youtube at my free time.Our mentors helped a lot in the entire course . I build a website for the first time....It was really a special moment...... I thought so now I became a great web builder. I am be like I can make any website without any other help. But slowly I realized that it is not a easy job .But my excitement increases with output and what to say? Now I also became a web developer not as too much but now I can build a website confidently.
It has instilled in us a sense of confidence and belief in our own abilities.Upskill has ignited a passion for technical studies, opened doors to new opportunities, and empowered us to make a difference in the world. In conclusion, I applaud the remarkable efforts of the TinkerHub Society and mentors who have made Upskill a transformative experience. Together, we have embraced the spirit of lifelong learning, innovation, and growth. Upskill has ignited a passion for technical studies, opened doors to new opportunities, and empowered us to make a difference in the world. Thank you, Upskill, for equipping us with the tools to succeed and for inspiring us to reach for the stars.Thankyou....
